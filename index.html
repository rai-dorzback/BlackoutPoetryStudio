Blackout Poetry Studio

Instructions

Blackout poetry is made by blacking out words on a pre-existing text so that the only ones which remain visible create a poem of your own.

  1. Get a base poem to work with. Either get a random poem, search for a poem by author, title, or keyword, or enter custom text.
  2. Click on the words you want to keep in your new poem.
  3. When you are ready, click 'Blackout'
  4. Save your poem by screenshotting! (In a future version you will be able to save directly from here.)
